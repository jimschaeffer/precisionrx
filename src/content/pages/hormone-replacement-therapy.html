Hormone Replacement Therapy Compounding in Vestavia Hills

Hormonal imbalance affects millions of people — and yet most commercial hormone medications offer only a handful of fixed doses and delivery forms. For many patients, that simply isn't enough. At Precision Rx Compounding in Vestavia Hills, we work with your physician to create bioidentical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT) formulated to your exact lab-tested levels — not an approximation.

Whether you're managing the effects of menopause, low testosterone, thyroid imbalance, or another hormonal condition, our compounding pharmacists collaborate directly with your prescriber to build a formulation that fits your biology, your lifestyle, and your treatment goals.

What Hormones Do We Compound?

Precision Rx compounds the three primary hormones at the center of most replacement therapy programs. Each can be prepared in a wide range of doses and delivery forms based on your physician's prescription and your individual needs.

Estrogen

Estrogen is the primary hormone targeted in female hormone replacement therapy, particularly for patients managing menopause symptoms such as hot flashes, sleep disruption, vaginal dryness, and mood changes. We compound estrogen as estradiol, estriol, or a combination of both, in forms including topical creams, transdermal gels, vaginal suppositories, and oral capsules — allowing your physician to prescribe the delivery method best suited to your physiology and symptom profile.

Progesterone

Progesterone is typically prescribed alongside estrogen in hormone replacement therapy to protect the uterine lining and support overall hormonal balance. Compounded progesterone allows for precise dosing that commercial products often cannot match, and can be prepared as oral capsules, topical creams, or vaginal suppositories. For patients with sensitivities to the synthetic progestins found in many commercial formulations, compounded bioidentical progesterone offers a well-tolerated alternative.

Testosterone

Testosterone therapy is prescribed for both men and women. In men, low testosterone — or hypogonadism — can cause fatigue, reduced libido, muscle loss, and cognitive changes. In women, low testosterone levels contribute to similar symptoms, particularly low energy and diminished sexual function. Compounded testosterone is available as topical creams, gels, and troches in the specific concentration your physician prescribes.

Why Choose Compounded BHRT?

Commercial hormone medications are manufactured in standardized strengths. For many patients, the dose they need falls between available options — or the delivery form simply doesn't suit their lifestyle or tolerability. Compounding solves this by allowing your pharmacist and physician to work together on a formulation that is:

  • Dosed to your specific lab results, not a population average
  • Prepared in the delivery form best suited to you — cream, capsule, troche, gel, or suppository
  • Free from dyes, fillers, or preservatives you may be sensitive to
  • Adjustable over time as your hormone levels and symptoms evolve
