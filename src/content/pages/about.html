About Precision RX Compounding Pharmacy

Vestavia Hills' Trusted Compounding Pharmacy

Precision Rx Compounding is an independent, locally owned compounding pharmacy serving patients and physicians across Vestavia Hills, Birmingham, Hoover, Homewood, and the surrounding communities since 2014. We exist for one reason: to provide medications that work for the individual — not the average.

When a commercially available drug isn't the right fit — whether the dose is wrong, the form is impractical, or an ingredient causes a reaction — we build a better solution from the ground up. Every medication that leaves our pharmacy is compounded by a licensed pharmacist using pharmaceutical-grade ingredients, prepared specifically for the patient whose name is on the prescription.

Serving Our Community Since 2014

What Makes Us Different From a Chain Pharmacy

At most chain pharmacies, you interact with a system. At Precision Rx, you interact with a pharmacist. When you call us, a licensed member of our compounding team answers. When you have a question about your medication, the person who made it can walk you through the answer. That level of direct, personal access is rare in modern pharmacy — and it's something we protect.

We don't have automated phone trees or a call center. We have a team of compounding professionals who know their patients by name and take personal responsibility for every formulation that goes out the door. That accountability is the foundation of everything we do.

Our Approach to Compounding

Compounding isn't a workaround — it's a precise, evidence-informed practice that requires specialized training, pharmaceutical-grade materials, and rigorous quality standards. At Precision Rx, every compound begins with a valid prescription from a licensed Alabama provider and is formulated to meet USP quality and safety standards. We source our active pharmaceutical ingredients exclusively from licensed, reputable suppliers, and our preparations are reviewed by a licensed pharmacist at every stage.

We work across a broad range of therapeutic areas, collaborating closely with the physicians, dermatologists, OB/GYNs, pediatricians, and other specialists who refer their patients to us. When a case is complex, we communicate directly with the prescriber — because getting it right for the patient matters more than a fast turnaround.

Therapeutic Areas We Serve

  • Hormone Therapy — bioidentical hormone replacement (BHRT) compounded to your lab-tested levels
  • Pain Management — targeted topical formulas that reduce systemic side effects
  • Dermatology — custom creams, gels, and solutions for skin conditions of all types
  • Pediatric Medicine — precise, flavored, allergen-free formulations for children
  • Women's Health — support across every stage of life, from fertility to menopause
  • Veterinary Medicine — custom compounds for pets when commercial options fall short

Serving Birmingham Since 2014

We opened our doors in 2014 with a straightforward mission: to be the compounding pharmacy that Birmingham-area patients and physicians could count on. Over the past decade, we've built lasting relationships with patients from across Jefferson and Shelby Counties and with a wide network of referring physicians who trust us with their most complex cases.

Our pharmacy is located at 3325 Rocky Ridge Plaza, Suite 207, Vestavia Hills, AL 35243 — conveniently accessible from Birmingham, Hoover, Homewood, Mountain Brook, and the surrounding area. We're open Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and our pharmacists are available by phone throughout the day.

Who We Work With

Precision Rx serves two audiences equally: the patients who depend on us for their medications, and the prescribers who refer those patients to us. Whether you're a patient looking for a medication that fits your needs or a physician searching for a reliable compounding partner in the Birmingham area, we're built to support you.

For Patients

If your current medication isn't working — because of the dose, the form, an ingredient sensitivity, or availability — talk to your doctor about compounding and ask them to send your prescription to Precision Rx. You can also contact us directly with questions before getting started.

For Prescribers

We accept prescriptions electronically, by fax, or in person. Our pharmacists are available for direct consultation on complex cases, alternative formulation options, and dosing questions. We take communication seriously and will always keep you informed throughout the compounding process.

Our Commitment to the Vestavia Hills Community

We're not a fulfillment warehouse or a remote operation. We're a neighborhood pharmacy — staffed by people who live and work in this community, who care about the patients who walk through our door, and who measure success by outcomes, not volume. That's what independent pharmacy looks like, and it's a standard we hold ourselves to every day.

If you'd like to learn more about Precision Rx or find out whether compounding is right for you or your patient, call us at (205) 823-0780 or stop by our Vestavia Hills location. We'd love to meet you.
