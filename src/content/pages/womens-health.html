Women's Health Compounding in Vestavia Hills

A woman's health needs change significantly across her lifetime — from reproductive years through perimenopause, menopause, and beyond. At each stage, the right medication in the right form can make a meaningful difference in quality of life. At Precision Rx Compounding, we prepare custom women's health medications for patients across Birmingham, Vestavia Hills, and the surrounding communities, working closely with OB/GYNs, gynecologists, and other specialists to support individualized care.

Women's Health Compounds We Prepare

Hormone Replacement Therapy

Compounded bioidentical hormone replacement therapy is one of our most frequently requested women's health formulations. We compound estrogen, progesterone, and testosterone in doses and delivery forms tailored to each patient's lab results and symptom profile. Whether you're managing hot flashes, night sweats, mood changes, or sexual dysfunction, a compounded BHRT formulation can be adjusted over time as your needs evolve in a way that commercial products simply cannot accommodate. Visit our Hormone Replacement Therapy page for full details.

Pelvic Floor Pain — Suppositories and Creams

Pelvic floor disorders, vulvodynia, vaginismus, and pelvic pain conditions are among the most underserved areas in women's health — and compounding plays an important role in their management. Precision Rx prepares custom vaginal suppositories and topical creams containing muscle relaxants, anti-inflammatories, anesthetics, and other active ingredients as prescribed by a pelvic health specialist or gynecologist.

These preparations deliver medication directly to the affected tissue, allowing for targeted relief with minimal systemic absorption. Compounded pelvic floor formulations are particularly valuable for patients who have not responded to commercially available options or who require a combination of ingredients not available in a single commercial product.

Menopause Support

Menopause brings a wide range of symptoms — many of which respond well to localized or systemic hormone support when commercial formulations aren't sufficient or well-tolerated. We compound estradiol, estriol, and progesterone preparations in the specific strengths and forms your physician prescribes, including vaginal creams for atrophic vaginitis, topical gels for systemic symptom management, and oral capsules for sleep and mood support.

Fertility Support

Women undergoing fertility treatment sometimes require compounded medications when commercial preparations are unavailable, when a specific dose isn't manufactured, or when an allergen-free formulation is needed. Precision Rx works with reproductive endocrinologists and fertility specialists to prepare progesterone suppositories, estradiol formulations, and other fertility-related compounds as part of a physician-directed treatment protocol.
